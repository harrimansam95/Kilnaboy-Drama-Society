How To Apply?

We are always looking for members who are interested in all aspects of drama from set design to acting and production. To apply contact Michael O'Hara directly or phone 0852164474. Answering the casting calls can also be directed to Michael O'Hara who will then take on the submission directly or you will be refered to the relivant department.

Where are we?

We are located in Kilnaboy, Co.Clare.

Where Can I buy tickets?

All productions that are local are held in the Kilnaboy hall, tickets are sold on the door when a production is going. Tickets for productions on a circuit are sold or pre-ordered from their respective locations
